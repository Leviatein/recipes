Cinnamon Cookies

Description

A delicious, buttery, cream chees sugar cookie swirled with cinnamon and finished with glaze

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour
  • Baking powder and baking soda
  • Salt
  • Granulated sugar, brown sugar, powdered sugar
  • Cinnamon
  • Unsalted butter
  • Cream cheese
  • Egg
  • Vanilla extract
  • Whole milk

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients then cream butter, sugar and cream cheese until fluffy
  2. Blend in egg and vanilla
  3. Mix in flour mixture
  4. Divide dough into two portions and shape into squares. chill until firm for rolling.
  5. Roll each portion into large rectangle on floured surface
  6. Bursh with butter and sprinkle with cinnamon sugar
  7. Snuggly roll into a log and then slice with dental floss
  8. Bake until nearly set then let cool completely
  9. Glaze with vanilla icing and let set
