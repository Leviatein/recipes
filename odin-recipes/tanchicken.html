Tandoori Chicken Recipe

Description

Tandoori Chicken is an indian dish that is marinated in yogurt and spices then roasted in a hot tandoor oven

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs bone in skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 small container plain greek yogurt
  • 2 Tbsp minced garlic
  • 2 Tbsp minced ginger
  • 2 Tbsp vegetable or olive oil
  • 2 Tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • Cilantro, for serving
  • Lost of spices: 1 Tbsp paprika, 2 tsp ground cumin, 2 tsp ground coriander, 1/2 turmeric, 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper, 1/4 tsp ground cardamom, salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Gently toast ground spices in skllet on medium heat for 1-2 minutes, stir frequently
  2. In large mixing bowl stir together greek yogurt, oil, lemon juice, spices, and season
  3. Add chicken, toss and caot, cover bowl with plastic wrap
  4. Transfer to refrigerator and let marinate
  5. Preheat grill to 425, brush with oil
  6. Grill chicken til thickest part is 165 degrees, about 10-12 per side bone in, 5-7 per side boneless
  7. Brush lightly with oil as needed keep exterior moist
  8. Let chicken rest before serving, garnish with cilantro
